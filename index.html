FAQ

Common questions

What is twtholders.trustwallet.com?
This is Trust Wallet's official Priority Support portal, exclusively for TWT holders. All Trust Wallet users can access the standard Help Center, but holding and locking 50+ TWT unlocks Priority Support — giving you faster responses, guaranteed 24h specialist access, and the AI Transaction Checker.
Do I need TWT to get any support from Trust Wallet?
No. All Trust Wallet users can access the Help Center and submit support requests for free. TWT holders who lock 50+ tokens simply receive Priority Support on top of that — faster responses and exclusive tools — as a benefit of participation in the ecosystem.
What does locking TWT mean? Can I get it back?
Locking TWT means temporarily staking your tokens inside Trust Premium to access exclusive benefits. Your TWT remains yours throughout — you just can't transfer or spend it while it's locked. You can unlock it at any time, but the tokens become available again after a minimum 3-day unlock period.
What is the AI Transaction Checker?
The AI Transaction Checker is an AI-powered tool that reviews any blockchain transaction and explains what happened in plain language. It identifies failed, stuck, or unusual transactions, shows you fee breakdowns, and tells you exactly what to do next — no blockchain expertise required.
Does Priority Support guarantee my issue will be resolved?
Priority Support guarantees that a specialist will respond to your ticket within 24 hours — not just an automated message. The team will always work to fully resolve your issue, but the 24h guarantee specifically covers the initial specialist response time.
Is Priority Support available in my country?
Priority Support is available globally to eligible TWT holders. Some options to buy or swap TWT may depend on your region and local provider availability, but the Priority Support feature itself is not geo-restricted.
How do I buy or get TWT if I don't have any?
Visit this page and connect a wallet with less than 50 TWT — you'll see a "Get TWT" button. On mobile, it redirects to the in-app swap screen where you can swap BNB, USDT, or USDC for TWT. On desktop, it routes through PancakeSwap on BNB Smart Chain. Add a small buffer for network fees.
